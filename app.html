Unthink: Code Challenge

Have you ever been counting and lost track of what number you were on? Ever wanted to know all the numbers between two values? We built a number counter for you so you never have to struggle again. We've taken all the heavy lifting out of numbers. We use Javascript loops and HTML properties to do the work behind the to count from 0-100 and display the output to you!

Instructions

Choose 2 values, a lower and a higher number, and click submit. The app will run it's brainy calculations and display the numbers on the screen.
